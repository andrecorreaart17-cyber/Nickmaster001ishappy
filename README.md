# Games

## Basket Random

Basket Random is a fun, chaotic basketball game where players can enjoy random layouts, quick matches, and humorous gameplay. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, the unpredictability ensures a unique experience every time.

### Features:
- **Simple Controls:** Easy to pick up and play.
- **Random Mechanics:** Every match offers a new surprise!
- **Multiplayer Mode:** Challenge your friends to beat your score.

Get ready to shoot some hoops and embrace the randomness!

## Getaway Shootout

Getaway Shootout is a fun and chaotic multiplayer racing game where you compete against other players to reach an extraction point or getaway vehicle first. The game is known for its simple but challenging controls, allowing players to jump left or right and use special weapons or power-ups picked up on the map.

### Features:
- **Four-Player Multiplayer:** Play with friends or against AI.
- **Chaotic Gameplay:** Race across levels filled with obstacles and power-ups.
- **Awkward Controls:** Intentionally challenging controls add to the fun and difficulty.
- **Fast-Paced Action:** First to reach the goal wins!

Jump, shoot, and dodge your way to victory!
